About Us

Who Are We?

Your Trusted Partner in Mining & Industrial Supplies.

Meetsi A Lewatle Projects is a dynamic, Black Youth Female-owned business proudly based in South Africa. We were founded on the belief that great service, quality products, and strong partnerships are the building blocks of successful operations — especially in demanding industries like mining and industrial manufacturing.

What sets us apart is not just what we supply, but how we work:

We are energetic, service-driven and deeply committed to our clients.

We understand the pressure our clients face – and we respond with agility,
professionalism, and purpose.

We are continuosly learning, growing and flowing with the change – so we can
better serve those who trust us.

As a proudly Black youth-owned enterprise, we’re also passionate about empowering others, contributing
to local development, and being part of a stronger, more inclusive economy.

What Do We Do?

We specialize in the supply of high-quality, cost-effective mining and industrial products — everything from safety equipment and electrical gear to mechanical parts and engineering consumables.

We serve businesses of all sizes and across sectors with products that meet performance, safety, and compliance standards. Our offering includes:

Electrical Components

such as motors, cables and solar solutions

Mechanical Essentials

including beerings, pumps and compressors

Engineering Consumables

such as PPE, welding gear, lubricants and more

Our team is here to simplify your procurement process, reduce stress, and
ensure you always have the right tools to keep your opreations running at full speed.
